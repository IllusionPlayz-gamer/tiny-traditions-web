Our Story

Handcrafted by Little Creators, With Big Hearts

Welcome to Tiny Traditions — where imagination, creativity, and kid-power come together to create something truly special.

We’re not just another boutique store — we’re a 100% kid-owned and kid-run setup. Every piece you see here is handcrafted by young makers who pour their heart and vision into every design.

We kids see the world with wonder, not limitations. That spirit lives in every Rakhi we create. Each one is thoughtfully designed and made by talented young hands, celebrating connection, craftsmanship, and culture.
